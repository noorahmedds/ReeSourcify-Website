The problem

Recycling is still too expensive because critical steps rely on guesswork.

Facilities handle complex device streams with manual dismantling, material sorting, and delayed reporting. Small workflow errors compound into lost labor hours, lower output purity, and weaker sales decisions.

62Mtonnes

of e-waste is produced globally every year, while only a fraction is recycled.

01

Slow dismantling

Every device is different. Workers lose time when they have no live guidance on tools, screws, components, or hazards.

02

Sorting errors

Wrong material in the wrong bin reduces purity and can heavily lower the value of recovered metal streams.

03

No real-time information

Facilities often learn throughput, purity, and recovery data weeks later — too late for volatile commodity markets.

The solution

Assist workers. Upgrade workflows. Capture intelligence earlier.

REESourcify combines computer vision hardware with a cloud software layer to make manual recycling workflows measurable, guided, and traceable.

✦

Dismantling Assistant

An overhead camera and monitor provide real-time instructions tailored to the device and the worker’s current step.

  • Tool recommendations
  • Component localization
  • Hazard detection
  • Verification and learning from experts
◉

Smart Bin

Retrofit bins with cameras, weight sensors, and lighting detect misplaced materials and create live recovery data.

  • Higher sorting accuracy
  • Material weight and purity insights
  • Instant alerts for contamination
  • Real-time dashboard visibility

Process intelligence

From device to material stream — visible in seconds.

REESourcify’s SaaS layer turns facility activity into operational insight: recovered material, average purity, throughput, contamination events, and worker-guided learning loops.
